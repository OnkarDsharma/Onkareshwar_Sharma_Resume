Who I Am

More than just code — the person behind the projects.

My Background

I'm a B.Tech student in Computer Science & Engineering at IIIT Naya Raipur (Class of 2028) with a deep passion for building things that matter. From AI-powered government service platforms to SaaS applications, I thrive at the intersection of machine learning and full-stack web development. My journey began with curiosity about how software can solve real-world problems — and it hasn't stopped since.

My Approach

I believe great software starts with understanding the problem deeply before writing a single line of code. I follow an iterative approach — build, measure, learn, and refine. Whether it's designing a three-tier storage architecture or fine-tuning a neural network, I focus on creating solutions that are robust, scalable, and user-centric. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries and deliver real impact.

I'm deeply passionate about painting, visual design, and creative expression — skills that uniquely inform my approach to UI/UX and product design.
